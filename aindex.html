About the movie

Godzilla vs Kong is a 2021 American monster film directed by Adam Wingard. It is a sequel to Godzilla: King of the Monsters and Kong: Skull Island, and the fourth film in Legendary's MonsterVerse.

The film follows the epic battle between Godzilla and Kong, as they both try to assert their dominance over the world. Along the way, they encounter a team of humans who try to uncover the mystery behind the Titans' existence.

The movie was released in theaters and on HBO Max on March 31, 2021, and it received positive reviews for its action scenes, special effects, and the performances of the cast.
